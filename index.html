The Atelier

Where Heritage Meets Modern Design

LOD Fashion Design is more than a boutique; it is a sanctuary for those who value individuality. Founded by Kavya Nair, our studio blends traditional Indian craftsmanship with contemporary silhouettes.

Each piece is meticulously handcrafted in our Bengaluru atelier, using the finest silks, linens, and hand-embroidered details. We believe that true luxury lies in the fit and the feeling of a garment made specifically for you.

Kavya Nair

Creative Director & Founder
